# VibeVault

VibeVault is a personal portfolio showcase for presenting high-quality projects built in the AI era with Codex and Claude.

## Language

**Personal Portfolio**:
A curated showcase centered on the creator's identity, taste, and project craft.
_Avoid_: AI project lab, experiment archive

**Project**:
A finished or presentable creation selected for public showcase.
_Avoid_: experiment, repository item

**AI Product Builder**:
The creator identity presented by the portfolio, emphasizing shipped AI-era products over narrow implementation roles.
_Avoid_: Creative AI Frontend Developer, Full-stack Developer

**Hero Statement**:
The first-screen message "VibeVault — AI Era Creations by an AI Product Builder."
_Avoid_: VibeVault — AI Product Builder

**Primary Navigation**:
The top-level portfolio navigation: VibeVault, Projects, About, Contact, and View Work.
_Avoid_: purely decorative navigation

**Hero Atmosphere**:
A 3D particle starfield with a low-contrast wireframe grid that creates depth without overpowering the portfolio message.
_Avoid_: cyber cityscape, generic AI neural network

**Brand-Led Hero**:
A first-screen presentation led by the VibeVault name, statement, navigation, and atmosphere rather than a portrait or personal logo.
_Avoid_: portrait-led hero, personal photo hero

## Relationships

- A **Personal Portfolio** contains multiple **Projects**.
- Each **Project** represents the creator's work and may link to a demo and source code.
- The **Personal Portfolio** presents the creator as an **AI Product Builder**.
- The **Hero Statement** combines the portfolio name with the creator identity.
- The **Primary Navigation** gives direct access to the portfolio sections and uses "View Work" as the main action.
- The **Hero Atmosphere** supports the **Hero Statement** without becoming the main content.
- The **Brand-Led Hero** keeps personal imagery out of the first screen.

## Example dialogue

> **Dev:** "Should VibeVault feel like an AI project laboratory?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — it is a **Personal Portfolio** first, with AI-era tooling as part of the story."
> **Dev:** "Should the Hero introduce the creator as a frontend specialist?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — the public identity is **AI Product Builder**, because the work should feel like shipped products."
> **Dev:** "Should the Hero be minimal and only say 'AI Product Builder'?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — use the full **Hero Statement** so the AI-era creation theme stays explicit."
> **Dev:** "Should the navigation be only section links?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — the **Primary Navigation** should include a 'View Work' action because this portfolio should feel productized."
> **Dev:** "Should the Hero use a neon city scene?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — use **Hero Atmosphere** so the first impression feels immersive and restrained."
> **Dev:** "Should the first screen include a personal photo?"
> **Domain expert:** "No — use a **Brand-Led Hero** and reserve personal introduction for the About section."

## Flagged ambiguities

- "VibeVault" could imply an AI project lab or archive; resolved: the canonical product concept is **Personal Portfolio**.
- The Hero identity could emphasize frontend craft or full-stack scope; resolved: use **AI Product Builder**.
- The Hero subtitle could be short or thematic; resolved: use **Hero Statement** with "AI Era Creations by an AI Product Builder."
- Navigation could be pure anchors or product-style; resolved: use **Primary Navigation** with a "View Work" action.
- Hero background could be city, neural network, or spatial abstraction; resolved: use **Hero Atmosphere**.
- Hero could include a portrait or personal logo; resolved: use **Brand-Led Hero** without personal imagery.
